Where the honey harvested from?

Our honey is harvested across different countries around the world. It includes Indonesia, Thailand, Malaysia at this moment. There will be more honey be introduced across the time.

How should I store my honey?

All the honey should be store in room temperature, shaded from direct sunlight to keep the honey at its best texture. Keep it away from places that would attract ants because honey contains natural glucose and fructose which the ants love it.

Why honey harden into crystal and how to do it?

Crystallization occurs because of the natural qualities inside. The natural sugars in honey (glucose and fructose) will bind together and begin to form little crystals, which can start making your honey harder. It is still edible even honey crystalize. If you need that flowing golden liquid, place crystallized honey in a warm water bath of about 40°C (100°F) for 15 minutes or more until the crystals dissolve and the honey liquefies.
