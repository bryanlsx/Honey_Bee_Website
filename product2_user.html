Classic White Honey

A product that is loved by many, you can never go wrong with our classic white honey. This product helps consumers with anti-aging and relaxing the mind and body. It is high in Vitamin B6 which also works as a natural remedy for insomnia by increasing the body's production of melatonin. Besides that, it is valued for its anti-aging benefits and also improves brain function and memory, reduces stomach and mouth ulcers and aids in healthy fetal development during pregnancy hence the reason why it is popular among pregnant womens..




The Classic White Honey is made by honeybees that hive at the coast, by the waters of Sumbawa Island in Sumatra, Indonesia.
