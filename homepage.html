Technology

How we produce our honey

Making honey is a complex and unique process
and is produces in several stages

Honey production has become high tech as new innovations are made. Modern bee hives are designed with remote hive monitoring systems that enable beekeepers to remotely track and optimize honey production.

Read More

01. Flowers produce nectar and attract our bees

Bees are particularly attracted to bee balm, echinacea, snap dragon, and hostas, as well as a number of other wildflowers like California poppies and evening primrose.

02. Bees collect the nectar and carry it to the beehive

How do bees collect nectar and carry it? Bees collect nectar from flowers. Nectar is the sweet liquid that entices the bees to the flower.

03. Bees seal cells with wax and honey ripens

When the water content is reduced to about 20 percent, the bees seal the cell with a wax capping.

04. We collect the product and transfer it to the packaging

After the honey is ready to be harvested, we will then harvest the honey and process it in order to be ready for packaging to be sold.
